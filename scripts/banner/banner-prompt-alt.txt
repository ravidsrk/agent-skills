A wide horizontal banner image, 16:9 landscape aspect ratio (1920×1080 feel), for the top of a GitHub README.

STYLE: Modern minimalist isometric illustration. Soft pastel + warm orange accent palette. Clean vector look, NOT photorealistic. NO heavy text, NO dense ASCII, NO terminal screens. Light, calm, approachable — the visual equivalent of a well-designed product landing page.

PALETTE:
  - Background: very soft cream / off-white (#F8F4EE) with a faint warm gradient from left (peach #FFE5D1) to right (sky cream #EEF0F5)
  - Primary accent: warm orange #F97316 (Tailwind orange-500) — used sparingly on highlighted shapes
  - Secondary: deep slate #1F2937 (line work, type)
  - Tertiary: muted teal #5EAAA8 (one or two element fills)
  - White (#FFFFFF) for card faces

LAYOUT:
  Centered composition. Six (6) isometric "skill cards" arranged in a gentle horizontal arc across the canvas, slightly overlapping. Each card is a thin floating rectangle in isometric perspective (think a credit-card-sized object floating in 3D space, 30° isometric angle).

  Each card represents one skill with a single iconic visual on its face:

  CARD 1 (leftmost, slight back): a stylized cloud icon with subtle DNS-record lines flowing through it. Cream face, orange accent stripe at the top. Label below in small caps: "CLOUDFLARE-DNS".

  CARD 2 (front-left): a small isometric envelope or scroll with a tag on it (Namecheap = domain registration). Cream face. Label: "NAMECHEAP-DNS".

  CARD 3 (CENTER, slightly larger and more forward, this is the hero): a stylized airplane or balloon (Fly.io) on the LEFT half of the card, with an arrow arching to small isometric server cubes (AWS) on the right half. This card is tilted slightly toward the viewer. Orange accent more prominent. Label: "FLY-TO-AWS-MIGRATION".

  CARD 4 (front-right): a central node with EIGHT thin lines fanning outward to small orbiting circles (representing the 8 research sources). Cream face. Label: "DEEP-RESEARCH".

  CARD 5 (right, slightly back): a stylized poster or framed picture with abstract pixel-bitmap art on it. Cream face. Label: "TERMINAL-POSTER".

  CARD 6 (rightmost, slight back): a stylized broom sweeping three tiny squares into a neat checked stack (issues being closed). Cream face, teal accent. Label: "CLEAN-SWEEP".

ABOVE the cards (top-left of canvas): the title "agent-skills" set in a clean modern sans-serif (Inter or Geist), dark slate, lowercase, comfortable size — about 1/8 the canvas height. Below the title, in smaller weight: "production-grade skills for AI agents".

BELOW the cards (bottom-center): a single thin horizontal line of tiny dots and an x/twitter handle "@ravidsrk" in muted gray small caps.

GENERAL FEEL: Linear.app meets Stripe.com meets a Dieter Rams product photo. Calm, confident, premium. Lots of negative space. The cards should feel like physical objects you could pick up. Subtle drop shadows under each card. NO dense text. NO terminal aesthetics. NO cyberpunk. NO neon glow. No overcomplicated patterns.

The viewer should immediately understand: "this is a curated collection of 6 distinct tools".
